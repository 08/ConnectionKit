Main content area

Every page is split into a number of distinct elements. Most of these appear on almost every page, such as the sidebar and Page Title.

The main content area of a page is that part of the page which is unique, it contains no "common" elements.

So, for a text page, the main content area is the text you write. For a photo page, it is the photo itself.
