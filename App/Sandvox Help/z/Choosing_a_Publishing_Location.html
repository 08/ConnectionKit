Choosing a Location

Sandvox allows you to publish to a remote Host, or to your own computer.

On Your Computer

Publishing to your computer is generally not suitable for a home setup. It requires the computer to be turned on all the time and accessible from the Internet. Ideally, your computer should also have a static IP address, but you can instead make use of a service such as Dynamic DNS.

On a Remote Host

Publishing to a remote Host works by uploading your website to a server on the Internet. You might buy this service from a hosting company, or use webspace provided by your ISP, workplace or academic institution.

You can choose between publishing to a .Mac account, or another host.

If publishing to another host, Karelia has a few companies that we recommend.
