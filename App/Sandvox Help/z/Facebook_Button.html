Facebook Button

You can easily add a Facebook 'Like' button to your site by adding a "Facebook button" object.


To add a Facebook button to your site. Choose "Facebook button" from the "Objects" menu in the toolbar.

You can customize how your Facebook object looks in the object inspector.

Location

This is the page that you want your visitors to like. It can be the page that the Facebook object is located on or another page that you type in manually.

Appearance

You can choose the layout:

  • Standard
  • Button-only, Tall
  • Button-only, Wide

Colors:

  • Light
  • Dark

Label:

  • Like
  • Recommend
