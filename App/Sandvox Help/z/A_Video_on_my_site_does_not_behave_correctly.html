A Video on my site does not behave correctly

Using the Inspector, you can adjust the behavior of a Video. For example, you can make the video loop, or hide the control strip.

However, you may find that when viewing your site, the Video doesn't quite match the Inspector settings. This is because Quicktime allows users to set their own individual computer settings, which can override those on your site. Therefore this issue is not generally something you can resolve yourself.
