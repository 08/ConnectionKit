Banner

Some site designs feature a banner; a large image across the top of the page. For example, the Crimson Royale design:


Sandvox allows you to replace the banner with one of your own choosing.

To set the banner image of a site:

  1. Open the Site Inspector.
  2. Go to the Media tab.
  3. Drag your new banner image to the box labeled "Banner." This can be from the Media Browser, the Finder, or another application.

Sizing

Banner images can be a maximum of 800 pixels wide and 300 pixels high.

  • If you try to use an image wider than 800 pixels, Sandvox will shrink it to that width.
  • If the image is taller than 300 pixels (even after shrinking), Sandvox will crop it to be 300 pixels high.


However, the actual size of the banner varies from design to design. In particular, height varies from about 150 to 300 pixels, with the standard size being 200 pixels. Furthermore, some designs may actually allow a larger area for the banner on the Home Page than other pages of the site.

Sandvox will attempt to fit your supplied banner image as well as it can. If this is not to your satisfaction, try cropping the image (using an application such as Preview) to a more appropriate size and using that.

Banner Image Sources

freewebpageheaders.com has a large number of images available for free, as long as your site is for non-commercial use and they are credited.

Of course, you can also use any photos you might have taken - just be sure to crop them appropriately first.
