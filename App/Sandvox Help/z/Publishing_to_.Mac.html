Publishing to .Mac

Previous Step

This article is part of the "Setting Up Your Host" procedure, and is for if you are publishing your site to Apple's .Mac service. The previous step of this process is "Choosing a Publishing Location."

Keychain Access

In order to publish to .Mac, Sandvox needs to know your .Mac member name and password. This information is kept in your Keychain. When Sandvox attempts to retrieve your details, the Keychain will ask for your permission:

Click "Always Allow" since Sandvox needs to access the information each time you publish.

Subfolder

Once Sandvox has access to your .Mac account, you need to choose if you wish to publish to a subfolder. Sandvox presents you with a pane like this:

If you wish to host multiple sites on your .Mac account, you need to store each one in a subfolder. Enter the subfolder for this site in the box provided. Note that you cannot use any unsuitable characters, such as spaces.

If you are only publishing one site, then simply leave the box blank.

iDisk

Sandvox publishes your site to the "Sites" folder in your iDisk. You can use this to view all the files that Sandvox has published or to delete old, unnecessary files.

Note that if you are using iDisk syncing, you won't see the changes to the Sites folder Sandvox makes until you next sync.
