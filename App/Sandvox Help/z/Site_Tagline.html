Site Tagline

The Site Tagline appears at the top of every page of a site, usually just below the Site Title.

To change the Site Tagline:

  1. Click the Site Tagline.
  2. Enter your new text.
  3. Press Return or click outside of the text area.

To show or hide the site Tagline:

Check or uncheck the 'Tagline' box on the Appearance tab of the Document Inspector.
