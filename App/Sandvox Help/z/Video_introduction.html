Video introduction

Sandvox features a video introduction to help you get started with creating a website.

To view the video requires an Internet connection of at least 64 kbps.

To view the Sandvox video introduction:

  1. Open the Help menu and go to "Watch Video Introduction."

or:

  1. View the screencast online at the Karelia website.

Note: Choosing "Watch Video Introduction" will open the video in QuickTime Player if possible. If the QuickTime Player cannot be opened, the video will appear in your web browser. (If you are using Safari; this video will start playing immediately; from Firefox, it will take a while to load before it can be played.)
