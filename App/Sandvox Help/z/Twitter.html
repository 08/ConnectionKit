Twitter

The Twitter object shows your Tweets from Twitter on your Sandvox page.

To add your Twitter feed:

  1. Add you Twitter username to the username box.
  2. Select the number of Tweets that you would like to be included on the page.

You can also add a timestamp, so your visitors know when that Tweet was posted; And choose to have your Tweets to open in a new window, so when they are clicked on your visitor is not taken away from your website.
