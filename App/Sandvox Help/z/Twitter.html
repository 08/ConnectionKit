Twitter

The Twitter object shows your website visitors some recent Twitter updates from you.

To add your Twitter feed:

  1. Add your Twitter username to the username box.
  2. Select the number of Tweets that you would like to be included on the page.

Twitter objects will only function if your account is not protected; you must have a public Twitter profile.

You can also add a timestamp, so your visitors know when that tweet was posted. You can also choose to have your tweets to open in a new window, so when they are clicked on your visitor is not taken away from your website.
