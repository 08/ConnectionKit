Removing a comment

This page refers to the legacy Haloscan commenting system. We are working with the folks at JS-Kit to improve their documentation of comment moderation.

Occasionally you may wish to remove a comment from your site, perhaps due to spam or offensive language.

To remove a comment:

  1. Log in to your Haloscan account.
  2. Go to the Manage Comments section of the "Members" area.
  3. Delete the offending post. If necessary, you can even ban the poster.

For more information on managing comments, please see the Haloscan Help.
