Registering Sandvox

If you choose to buy your Sandvox license from our online store (rather than within the application), you will need to register Sandvox yourself. Also, if you move computer or have unregistered Sandvox at any point, you need to re-register it.

If you have paid for Sandvox within the application interface itself (i.e. from the Buy/Register Sandvox menu), the program should be automatically registered.

To register your Sandvox license:

  1. Locate your Sandvox license key. When you first purchased Sandvox, Karelia will have sent this to you in an e-mail. If you can't find your license key, please see "Finding your license key."
  2. Choose "Buy/Register Sandvoxâ€¦" from the Sandvox menu.
  3. Copy your license key and paste it into the box at the top of the registration window.
  4. Click the "Register" button.
