Manually Uploading a Site Using Cyberduck

Cyberduck is a free, open source FTP client. You can use Cyberduck to upload a Sandvox site after exporting it to a folder on your computer.


To upload an exported Sandvox site using Cyberduck:

  1. Launch Cyberduck and click the "Open Connection" item in the toolbar.
  2. Select a protocol that your host supports. We prefer SFTP as it more secure, but if not FTP will work fine.

  3. Enter your hostname into the "server" field. Also enter your username and password. If you do not know any of this information, contact your host for it.

  4. Click the "Connect" button.

  5. Once Cyberduck has connected to your host, browse to and double-click your document root folder. If you do not know which folder this is, please check the documentation for your host (and if necessary, contact their technical support staff).
