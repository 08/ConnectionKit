Raw HTML object examples

There are many websites that provide snippets of HTML that you can include into your own website. As long as the content they provide is no wider than the container page. For example, the sidebar allows objects up to 200 pixels wide, so your object would need to be 200 pixels or less in width.


Some possible ideas:

  • From PayPal, a "buy now" or "donate" button
  • A Google Gadget such as a search form, video, clock or calendar, driving directions, map, horoscope, to-do list, word of the day, etc.
  • Google Adwords or Adsense
  • Embed a video from one of the many video services, such as Vimeo, Viddler, DailyMotion, Blip.tv, etc.
  • Pandora Music List
  • Last.fm
  • Squidoo Lens
  • Weather.com conditions/forecast
