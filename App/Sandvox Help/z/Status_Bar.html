Status Bar

The Status Bar appears at the bottom of every document window. It serves two purposes.

Firstly the Status Bar displays informational text about the item the mouse is hovering over. So, if you hover over a link to the Home Page, the Status Bar will display "Go to Home." Or, if you hover over an Editing Marker (), the text will read "Click on + to insert an element."

The second purpose of the Status Bar is to inform you of application information. At the right-hand end of the bar, "badges" may appear, alerting you to things like Sandvox news and updates.

To show or hide the Status Bar:

  1. Open the View menu.
  2. Choose "Show Status Bar" or "Hide Status Bar."
