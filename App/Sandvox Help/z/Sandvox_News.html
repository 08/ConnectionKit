Sandvox News

Sandvox has a built-in newsreader for fetching the latest Sandvox related news from the Karelia website.

To access the latest Sandvox news:

  1. Open the Help menu.
  2. Choose "Latest Sandvox News."

You can also read Sandvox news items from our website.

If you have it set in your Preferences, Sandvox will automatically check for new items periodically. When one is available, a small badge will appear in the Status Bar alerting you.
