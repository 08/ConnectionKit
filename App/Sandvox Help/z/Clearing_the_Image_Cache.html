Clearing the Image Cache

Sandvox keeps a cache of the images used in a site for performance reasons. When upgrading to a new Sandvox version, you may wish to clear the cache.

To clear the image cache:

  1. Open Sandvox's Preferences window.
  2. Change the setting in the "Preferred Format" popup button to the other option.
  3. Change the popup's setting back again.
  4. Close the Preferences window.
