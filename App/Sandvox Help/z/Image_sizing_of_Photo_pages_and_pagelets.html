Image sizing of Photo pages and pagelets

The width of Photo pages and pagelets are limited to a maximum width of 640 pixels for pages and 200 pixels for pagelets. This is to ensure that the image fits within the limits of a site's design. There is no limit on height.

When you create a Photo page or pagelet with an image that is larger than this size limit, Sandvox automatically resizes the image to fit.

Linking to Larger Images

Although the image size is restricted to fit in a page, you can still link to a larger, full-size version of your image.

To link to the full-size version of a Photo page or pagelet:

  1. Select the Photo page or pagelet.
  2. Open the Selection Inspector.

  3. Check the "Link To" box and select "Full-size image."
