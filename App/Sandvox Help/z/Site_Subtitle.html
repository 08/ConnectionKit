Site Subtitle

The Site Subtitle appears at the top of every page of a site, usually just below the Site Title.

To change the Site Subtitle:

  1. Click the Site Subtitle.
  2. Enter your new text.
  3. Press Return or click outside of the text area.

If you enter nothing, an Editing Marker () appears in place of the subtitle.
