Karelia Community Forum

Karelia maintains a web-based Community Forum, where users are encouraged to submit problems, questions, and suggestions. In this web-based community, Sandvox users help other Sandvox users in a timely manner.

To visit the Community Forum when you're working in the program, go to the Help menu and select "Visit Community Forum."

  • Visit the Karelia Community Forum

Karelia staff monitor this group to file feature requests appropriately, provide help, and track problems. If you need to contact Karelia Support to obtain a case number and get direct help from Karelia engineers, there is an item for this in the forum's site menu.
