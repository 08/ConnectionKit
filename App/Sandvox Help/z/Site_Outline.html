Site Outline

The Site Outline displays the pages of a site in a hierarchical manner. For Photo pages, Sandvox automatically places a thumbnail of the page's image inside the icon in the Site Outline.

The Site Outline is located at the left of all document windows.

To work with a page, you select it in the Site Outline and it appears in the Web View. You can also select multiple pages in the Site Outline.

Ways to select multiple pages in the Site Outline:

  • Hold down the Command key and click to select or deselect a page.
  • Hold down the Shift key and click a page. All pages between the previous selection and the page you click are highlighted.

This is useful if you want to drag multiple pages at once, or to use the "Group as Collection" command.

A site's Home Page always appears at the top of the Site Outline and is a collection that contains all other pages of the site. Other collections are indicated by the disclosure triangle next to them. Click the triangle to hide or show the pages within the collection.

Several kinds of pages can be created by dragging items into the Site Outline. For example, you can drag an image from the Media Browser into the Site Outline to create a Photo page.

You can also use the Site Outline to arrange or rearrange the structure of a site. For more information, please see "Arranging the pages of a site."

View Settings

To show or hide the Site Outline:

  1. Open the View menu.
  2. Choose "Show Site Outline" or "Hide Site Outline."

If you are working on a large site, you can set the Site Outline to use small page icons.

To use small page icons in the Site Outline:

  1. Open the View menu.
  2. Choose "Small Page Icons."
