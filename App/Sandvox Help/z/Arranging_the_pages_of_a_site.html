Arranging the pages of a site

The Site Outline is used to arrange the pages of a site. The position of the pages defines their order in both the Site Menu and any indexes.

To move a page around, simply drag its icon in the Site Outline. While you are dragging, a black line appears to indicate where the page will be placed once released:

If you hover over a Collection for a moment when dragging, it will expand to show its contents. This allows you to easily move a page into a Collection, or from one Collection to another.

Troubleshooting

You may find that you can't drop a page where you want to in the Site Outline. This is because the Collection you are trying to place it in is being automatically sorted. (Don't forget that the Home Page is a Collection too!)

To stop the Collection from being automatically sorted:

  1. Select the Collection in the Site Outline.
  2. Open the Page Inspector.
  3. Use "Collection" disclosure triangle to reveal all of the Collection settings.
  4. Set the "Sorting" popup to "Not sorted."

You can now place pages in any order you like within the Collection.
