Site Title

This name of a site. The Site Title appears across the top of every page of a site. Visitors can click on the Site Title to be taken to the Home Page.

To set the Site Title:

  1. Select the Home Page in the Site Outline.
  2. Click the Site Title text.
  3. Enter your new title and press Return.

If no text is entered, an Editing Marker () appears in the title's place.

If your site's title is already part of the logo or banner images, you can use the Site Inspector to hide the site title.
