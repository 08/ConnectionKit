Site Title

This name of a site. The Site Title appears across the top of every page of a site. Visitors can click on the Site Title to be taken to the Home Page.

To set the Site Title:

  1. Select the Home Page in the Site Outline.
  2. Click the Site Title text.
  3. Enter your new title and press Return.

If no text is entered, an editing marker () appears in the title's place.

Deleting the Site Title

It is possible to remove the Site Title simply by deleting all of its text. You can re-enter it by clicking the green editing marker () left in its place. However, we do not generally recommend this as it leaves search engines (e.g. Google) unable to distinguish the title of your website.

Instead, you can enter the Site Title as per normal, but then use the Site Inspector to hide the site title. The title is still present for search engines and people with accessibility needs to find, but invisible to normal visitors. This is also useful if your site's title is already part of the logo or banner images.
