Comments

Sandvox uses Haloscan to provide site visitors with the ability to leave comments on your site. This system also allows the use of Trackbacks.

To enable comments on your site:

  1. Sign up for a Haloscan ID. This is free and easy to do.
  2. In Sandvox, open the Site Inspector and go to the Properties tab.
  3. Enter your Haloscan username into the "Haloscan ID" box.

Additionally, you can enable Trackbacks by checking the "Also enable trackbacks" box.

You must then enable comments for each page you wish to allow them on. (Sandvox automatically enables comments for certain pages such as those in a blog.)

To enable comments for a particular page:

  1. Select the page in the Site Outline.
  2. Open the Page Inspector.
  3. Check the "Enable reader comments" box.
