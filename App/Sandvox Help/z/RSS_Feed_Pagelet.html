RSS Feed Pagelet

Many websites provide an RSS feed allowing people to subscribe to it. An RSS Feed pagelet takes such a feed and displays the headlines inside a pagelet on your site. Visitors can click on an item in the pagelet to read the full article.

Editing

To edit an RSS feed pagelet, you use the Selection Inspector. In addition to those listed below, there are some general pagelet attributes that can be set.

Also, don't forget that you can insert additional text into the pagelet by clicking the Editing Marker ().

Feed URL

The full URL of the RSS feed. For information on how to find the URL of a feed, please see the "RSS Feed" article.

Maximum number of entries

Limits the number of items displayed in the pagelet. If you enter "0" there will be no limit.
