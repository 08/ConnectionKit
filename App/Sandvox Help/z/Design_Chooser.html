Design Chooser

The Design Chooser allows you to browse and select a design for your site.


To select the design for a site, simply click a design in the Design Chooser, then click the 'choose' button.

To navigate the Design Chooser you use the arrows located to the left to scroll up and down.

Some designs have more that one color variation, in the top left corner of the design in the design chooser you will see the number of variations if any. To view the variations, roll the mouse over the design or click the corner where it says the number of variations.
