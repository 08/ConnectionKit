Sandvox is not registered properly

Karelia offers Sandvox as a free trial. While running as a trial, there are some limitations imposed:

  • You can only publish a site's Home Page. All other pages are not published and links to them are broken.
  • A large yellow banner will appear across the site with the wording "This page was created by a trial version of Sandvox."
  • A red badge labeled "Buy Sandvox" appears in the Status Bar.

To publish the entire site correctly, you need to buy and register Sandvox.

First make sure you have purchased Sandvox. You can do this from within the application, or from out online store. If you pay through the online store, you also need to register Sandvox.

If you are still having issues, try registering Sandvox again as detailed in "Registering Sandvox." If that doesn't work, please contact us.
