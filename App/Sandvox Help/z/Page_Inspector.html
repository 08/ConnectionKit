Page Inspector

The Page Inspector is split into three tabs. The first tab is the page tab and contains settings that are applicable to publishing, navigation, meta tags / keywords, and the time and date. The second tab is the appearance tab, that is where you can choose the page layout and sidebar items. The third tab is the collection tab and allows you to convert a single page to a collection, or inspect the settings for an existing collection..
