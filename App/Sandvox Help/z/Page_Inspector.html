Page Inspector

The Page Inspector is split into three tabs:

  1. Page contains settings that are applicable to publishing, navigation, meta tags / keywords, and the time & date
  2. Appearance is where you can adjust the page's layout, including sidebar items
  3. Collection handles collection settings — sorting, RSS, archives — and converting between single page & collection
