Upgrading Documents Created by Older Versions of Sandvox

When upgrading an older document for use with Sandvox 2, Sandvox will automatically convert it to a Sandvox 2 document and give it a .sandvox extension.
Your older Sandvox document will remain in place with the current .svxSite extension.
