Background Music

You can easily add background music to a single page or a whole collection of pages in your site.

To add background music:

  1. Add a new Video pagelet to the pages you wish to have the background music. You can add the Video to a Collection page (such as the Home Page) in order for the music to play on every page in the Collection.
  2. Select this new pagelet.
  3. Open the Selection Inspector and set the Video to use the appropriate file for background music.

If you have added the Video pagelet to a Collection page, you can also check the "Show on child pages" box in the Inspector to have the music play on all the pages in the Collection.

To have the background music automatically play when the page is opened, check the "Automatically play" box in the Selection Inspector.

You may wish to have the background music play continuously. To do so, check the "Loop" box in the Inspector.
