Photo

There are three ways to incorporate a photo in a Sandvox site: on a Photo page, on a Photo object, or as an Embedded Image on a text page.

Photo pages and objects are specialized: they display a single photo, but they are not meant to incorporate large amounts of text together with photos. Photo pages are often used together to make a Photo Album.

If you want to include an image within some text, then a Photo page or object is not for you. Instead, you want to create an Embedded Image within a text page.

A Photo page.
A Photo object.


Photo pages and objects support a wide range of image formats. For more information, please see "Images".

Ways to add a Photo to a site:

  • Use the "New" or "Objects" items in the toolbar.
  • Choose "New Page" or "More" from the Insert menu.
  • Drag an image (from the Media Browser, Finder or another application) to the Site Outline or Web View.

If you create a Photo page or object by dragging an image from iPhoto, Sandvox will incorporate information about the Photo if it is available, including the Photo's title, caption and any tags.

Photo pages and objects are limited in their width in order to accommodate the size of the site design. For more information, please see "Image Sizing."

Settings

To edit a Photo page:

  1. Select the page in the Site Outline.
  2. Open the Page Inspector.
  3. Adjust any settings.


To edit a Photo object:

  1. Click the object to select it.
  2. Open the Inspector.
  3. Adjust any settings.

Source

Two options are available here: "From file" and "From URL."

  • In most cases, you will add photos from files on your computer. Under "File Info" click "Choose" to browse to the location of the the photo file you want to display on the page.
  • The "From URL" option allows you to use an image hosted on another website. Enter the address of the image. Both copyright law and common courtesy suggest that you should not use this option unless you have permission from the hosting site, so please do not "hijack" images from other websites.

Alternate Text

Type here the text that you want to display for for any visitors who cannot see the image. Please see "Accessibility" for further information.

Link To

In the 'Link To' Inspector you can make the photo becomes a link. There are many choices here.

  • Choosing "Link to external URL" will turn the photo itself into a link; when visitors click on the photo, they will be taken to the URL you specify here.
  • You can make the image an Email link, when visitors click on the photo they will be taken to their preferred email program to write an email.
  • Page in this site gives you the option to link to another page in your Sandvox site, to do that just drag the target to a page in the site outline.
  • Selecting "Link to full-size image" will allow the visitors to click the image to view a full sized image. You can choose for the full size image to open in a new window.
