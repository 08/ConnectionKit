Document Inspector

The Site Inspector is one segment of the Inspector. It is used to adjust properties and settings of the whole site.

To open the Document Inspector:

  1. Click the "Inspector" item in the toolbar or choose "Show Inspector" from the View menu.

  2. Select the "Document" segment.

The Site Inspector contains two tabs. The Site tab and the Appearance tab.

Site

On the Site tab you will find. A box to write you name as the author of your website. A popup menu to select the language of your site.

A comments section to configure the comments if you would like to use for your site. Those include: Disqus, IntenseDebate, or Facebook.

The date format. Character encoding options.

And a Google button used to set up Google Integration

Appearance

Here you will find options specific to the appearance of your Sandvox document.

Layout, including title, tagline, footer, and logo. Banner, where you would use the "Image-Fill" options to choose a custom banner. Favicon, the small icon that appears next to your web address in the address bar of your browser, when the site is viewed online. Graphical Titles, In some deigns the Title can be replaced with a graphical title. And you image options, for scaling and sharpening images.
