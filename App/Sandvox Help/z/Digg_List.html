Digg List

A Digg list is a pagelet that displays a list of stories from Digg. A typical Digg list looks something like this:

Editing

To edit a Digg list, you use the Selection Inspector. In addition to the attributes listed here, there are some general pagelet attributes that can be set.

Also, don't forget you can click an Editing Marker to insert some additional text into the pagelet.

Stories

Select the stories that appear in the pagelet. "Promoted stories" lists only those articles that other people have favored, while "Submitted stories" will display all stories submitted to Digg.

The third option is "Only stories submitted by." Enter the username of a Digg member and choose whether you want stories that have been commented on, submitted by, dugg, or on the homepage of this user.

A good use of this is if you are a keen Digg user. Set the Digg list to show only stories dugg by yourself, and visitors to your site will be able to see what news you are interested in.

Category

Limits the stories to only those in the selected category.

Number to show

The number of articles from Digg to list.

Show descriptions

If checked, the descriptions attached to each story are also displayed. This will make the pagelet take up considerably more room.
